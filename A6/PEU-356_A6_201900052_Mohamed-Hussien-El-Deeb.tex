\subsection{Solution}

\]

    U x U^\dagger = x'
\]

\[
    U p U^\dagger = p'
\]

\[
    [x', p'] =U x U^\dagger U p U^\dagger - U p U^\dagger U x U^\dagger
\]

    = U x p U^\dagger - U p x U^\dagger = U [x, p] U^\dagger = U i U^\dagger = i
